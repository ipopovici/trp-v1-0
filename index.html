About us.

We founded THE ROOT PROJECTS with one goal in mind: providing a high-quality, smart, and reliable cafe. Our passion for excellence has driven us from the beginning, and continues to drive us into the future. We know that every product counts, and strive to make the entire experience as rewarding as possible.

The café.

More than a coffee shop…

Our mission is to build a sustainable and community friendly Cafe. By sourcing our ingredients locally, work with local garden centres to select the healthiest plants and most importantly giving back to the community through our community project. We aim to give our guests a place to celebrate life’s special moments by offering an experience like nowhere else.

The Root Projects has created a number of special moments for our guests, including a community day event which attracted hundreds of guests. We have had the pleasure to celebrate birthdays, national holidays and many more occasions with our friends. We hope that once you’ve experienced the magic of our ‘urban jungle’ cafe, you feel inspired to green your home and keep coming back for more!

The plants.

fern

Ferns are plants that do not have flowers. ... Similar to flowering plants, ferns have roots, stems and leaves. However, unlike flowering plants, ferns do not have flowers or seeds; instead, they usually reproduce sexually by tiny spores or sometimes can reproduce vegetatively, as exemplified by the walking fern.

strelitzia

Bird-of-paradise flower, (Strelitzia reginae), also called crane flower, ornamental plant of the family Strelitziaceae native to South Africa. The plant is grown outdoors in warm climates and as a houseplant for its attractive foliage and unusual flowers.

palm

Most palms are a straight, unbranched stem, but sometimes a branching stem, or even a creeping vine, such as the rattan. They have large evergreen leaves that are either 'fan-leaved' (palmate) or 'feather-leaved' (pinnate) and arranged in a spiral at the top of the trunk.

begonia

Begonia is a genus of about 1,300 species of annuals, perennials, shrubs and climbers. ... It is a compact, bushy, mounded, fibrous-rooted plant that features fleshy stems, waxy dark green to bronze leaves and loose clusters (cymes) of single or double flowers in shades of white, pink or red plus bicolor versions thereof.

alocasia

Alocasia, popularly called Elephant's ear or Kris plant, is a genus of tuberous or rhizomatous perennials from the Araceae family. ... This popular flowering plant is a beautiful and striking houseplant, with green leaves accentuated by light green or whitish leaf veins.

monstera

Monstera deliciosa, commonly called split-leaf philodendron, is native to Central America. It is a climbing, evergreen perennial vine that is perhaps most noted for its large perforated leaves on thick plant stems and its long cord-like aerial roots. ... Indoor plants rarely flower and fruit however.
